About me

Hi, I'm Woorak Choi. I grew up in South Korea, where I completed my Ph.D. in 2024 at Yonsei University with my supervisor Aeree Chung.

I'm interested in studying the Giant Molecular Clouds (GMCs), ISM, and magnetic field in extreme environments utilizing radio telescopes, ALMA and VLA.

I'm using JWST NIRCam data to investigate star formation in nearby galaxies.

I'm also using the simulation data (e.g., TIGRESS simulation) to study the same topics above.

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at McMaster University, Canada, working with Prof. Christine Wilson (Since Sep 2024).
